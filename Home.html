SKILLS

Dubki

One of the more famous offensive skills in kabaddi, the dubki is a move where the raider has to crouch low, spot an opening amongst a sea of defenders and thrust himself through the smallest of gaps with force to escape the chain of defenders.Pardeep Narval is the famous player having dubki skill.

Frog Jump

A frog jump takes place when a raider leaps high off the ground by thrusting himself off the incoming sea of defenders and reaching the midline. To execute the jump, one requires perfect timing and physical strength while also maintaining the correct body posture when landing on the ground.

KICK

Back kick, sidekick, and curve are the various types of kicks used by raiders. This can be used while maintaining distance from the antis, to confuse the defense. This is a good skill to be used when defense players are less in number.Back kick, sidekick, and curve are the various types of kicks used by raiders.

ANKLE HOLD

An ankle hold is a skill that helps a defender pin down a raider instantly by restricting his foot movements on the mat. The defender must judge when to attempt the move while a raider fishes for points with his feet.

BLOCK

As the name of this tackle implies, the defender uses his body to block the path of the raider in order to prevent him from escaping back to the raiding team's side. The block is typically used by the Covers and Corners.

Chain Tackle

Chain Tackle, is when two or more defenders coordinate among themselves and form a chain to block the raiders path and then execute one of the holds to stop him in his tracks. The chain system in Kabaddi is an advanced defence skill used



About PKA

Welcome to Pro Kabaddi Academy, where we foster the spirit of teamwork, discipline, and excellence through the sport of Kabaddi. Our academy is dedicated to providing quality coaching and training to aspiring Kabaddi players of all ages.

Rules and Conditions

We prioritize safety and fair play in our academy. All participants are expected to follow our academy's rules and guidelines to ensure a respectful and productive learning environment. We encourage our students to embrace sportsmanship and uphold the values of Kabaddi.

Aim of the Academy

Our aim is to promote Kabaddi as a sport and to nurture young talents who are passionate about the game. We aim to instill a sense of dedication, leadership, and camaraderie among our students. By focusing on both physical fitness and mental discipline, we aim to produce well-rounded athletes and individuals.
